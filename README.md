# AgroConnect (Harvest Hub)
AgroConnect is a community-driven platform designed to connect local urban farmers and growers with their neighborhood consumers. By facilitating direct peer-to-peer sharing and trading, the platform reduces food waste and strengthens local food sustainability.

# 🚀 Key Features
Modern Authentication: Beautifully designed Login and Sign-Up flows featuring custom elliptical and inverted-path headers for an immersive brand experience.

Geographical Discovery: An interactive map to find surplus harvests and community gardens within your immediate vicinity.

Produce Scanner: Integrated AI scanning tools to help growers identify and categorize their produce quickly.

Community Feed: A real-time marketplace for browsing local listings and sharing garden updates.

Direct Messaging: Secure, real-time chat to coordinate produce pickups and trades.
